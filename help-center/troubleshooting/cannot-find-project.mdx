---
title: Cannot Find My Project
description: Troubleshoot when your project does not appear.
---

## Symptoms

- Project missing on dashboard
- Unable to access documentation

## Steps to Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh the page">
    Press `Ctrl+R` or click reload in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    Confirm you are logged in to the correct account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If issue persists, email `support@stephenrobles.com` with your project name.
  </Step>
</Steps>
