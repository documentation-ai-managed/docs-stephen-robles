---
title: Organize Your Documentation
description: Quick guide to structuring your project documentation.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    Click "New Project" on the dashboard and enter the project name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add pages">
    Use the "Add Page" button to create sections for your docs, e.g., Overview, API, Guides.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrange order">
    Drag and drop pages in the order you want them to appear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite collaborators">
    Click "Invite" and enter their email to work together.
  </Step>
</Steps>
